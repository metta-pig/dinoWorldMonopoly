# Playtest plan — classroom (ages 7–10)

## Facilitator goals

- Confirm a full game finishes in **30–40 minutes** with 3–4 children.
- Check reading level on cards and board (2nd–3rd grade).
- Watch for confusion on **even building** and **full biome** rent bonus.

## Pre-session (5 min)

1. Read the **Quick reference** in RULES.md aloud.
2. Demo one turn: roll → land on empty habitat → buy → end turn.
3. Assign player names; start with **$12** each.

## Scenarios

### A — First purchase (10 min)

| Step | Check |
|------|-------|
| 2 players, young reader partners with adult | Can they identify biome color and price? |
| Land on Swamp habitat | Buy vs pass understood? |
| Land on owned habitat | Visit fee paid without argument? |

**Pass if:** both players complete 3 turns without facilitator explaining rules twice.

### B — Full biome + nest (15 min)

| Step | Check |
|------|-------|
| One player buys both Swamp habitats | Do they notice doubled base visit fee? |
| Attempt to build 2 nests on one habitat only | Facilitator enforces even building — is rule clear? |

**Pass if:** player builds at least one nest legally.

### C — Tar Pit and cards (10 min)

| Step | Check |
|------|-------|
| Send one player to Tar Pit via Ranger Outpost | No Hatchery $2 on the way? |
| Draw Fossil Find movement card | Movement direction clear? |
| Pay $1 or roll doubles to exit | Choice understood? |

### D — Endgame (remaining time)

| Step | Check |
|------|-------|
| Play until first bankruptcy or time limit | Game feels fun, not punitive? |
| Time limit variant | Net worth tie-break explained? |
