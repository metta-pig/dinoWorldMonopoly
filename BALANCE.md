# Balance notes

## Starting economy (kid-friendly small dollars)

| Players | Cash each | Total habitat value | Routes |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|--------|
| 2–4 | **$12** | $27 (habitats) + $8 (routes) | 2 × $4 |

**Hatchery pass/land:** +**$2**

All amounts stay in the **$1–$5** range so kids can count change without hundreds.

## Habitat prices and visit fees

| Habitat | Biome | Price | Base visit | Full biome | 1 nest | 2 nests | Exhibit |
|---------|-------|-------|------------|------------|--------|---------|---------|
| Parasaurolophus Pond | Swamp | $1 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $5 |
| Pteranodon Marsh | Swamp | $1 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $5 |
| Velociraptor Dunes | Desert | $2 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $6 |
| Ankylosaur Ridge | Desert | $2 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $6 |
| Stego Meadow | Forest | $2 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $6 |
| Triceratops Grove | Forest | $2 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $7 |
| Brachiosaurus Heights | Forest | $3 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $8 |
| Spinosaurus Bay | Ice | $4 | $1 | $2 | $2 | $4 | $8 |
| T-Rex Valley | Ice | $5 | $2 | $4 | $3 | $6 | $10 |

**Full biome:** owning every habitat in a biome doubles the **base** visit fee before nest/exhibit bonuses.

**Migration routes:** 1 owned → **$1** visit; 2 owned → **$2** visit. Price **$4** each.

**Building:** Nest **$1** (max 2); Exhibit **$3** (requires 2 nests, replaces them).

## Fixed fees

| Event | Amount |
|-------|--------|
| Visitor Center Fee (space 4) | Pay **$2** |
| Tar Pit exit | Pay **$1** or roll doubles |
| Ranger Outpost | Go to Tar Pit (space 9) |

## Card deck balance (8 + 8)

**Fossil Finds:** +$1–$2 from bank, $1 payments, movement, 2× Get Out of Tar Pit.

**Park Events:** +$1–$2 per player or from bank, Go to Tar Pit ×1, $1 repairs.

## Suggested physical bank (per player)

| Bill | Count |
|------|-------|
| $1 | 8 |
| $2 | 3 |
| $5 | 1 |

Starting total: $12 + $5 = enough bills for change during play.

## Target session length

- **30–40 min** at 2–3 players with kid turn pacing.
- Lower prices → faster buys and more frequent (but smaller) visit fees.

## Simulation

Run `npm run balance:sim` after rule changes. Target: p95 game length under 120 turns (2-player bots).
